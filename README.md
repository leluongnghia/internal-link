# AI Internal Linker

Automatically builds internal links using artificial intelligence. This plugin scans your WordPress content, discovers relevant internal posts, and naturally weaves them into your articles to boost your on-page SEO and improve user engagement.

## Features

- **Automated Internal Linking:** No more manual link building; let AI handle the heavy lifting.
- **Natural Weaving:** Seamlessly integrates links into your content exactly where they make structural and contextual sense.
- **SEO Optimization:** Boosts your site architecture and improves crawlability by building a strong internal link graph.
- **Customizable:** Configure how and where the internal links are placed.

## Installation

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/internal-links` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the **'Plugins'** screen in WordPress.
3. Navigate to the plugin's settings page to configure your AI credentials and linking preferences.

## Usage

After activation and configuration, the plugin will automatically process your posts based on your settings, scanning for contextual opportunities to interlink your site's articles.
